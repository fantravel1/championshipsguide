The Basics

What Is ChampionshipsGuide?

We're the world's most comprehensive guide to championships across every sport.

We catalogue, rate, and explain 200+ championships — from the FIFA World Cup to the World Darts Championship. Every championship gets a detailed guide covering its history, format, qualification process, and significance, plus a tier rating that tells you exactly where it sits in the global hierarchy of sporting events.

Whether you're a hardcore fan looking for travel tips to the Champions League final, or a casual viewer wondering what the difference is between the World Cup and the Euros — we've got you.

Our Rating System

How Our Tier System Works

We rate every championship on a 5-tier scale based on global viewership, historical significance, competitive depth, cultural impact, and prestige. Here's a quick overview.

Tier 1

Pinnacle

The absolute peak. Global audience in the billions, held every four years, transcends sport itself.

e.g. FIFA World Cup, Olympics

Tier 2

Elite Global

World-class events with massive global followings and the deepest competitive fields.

e.g. Champions League, Wimbledon, Rugby World Cup

Tier 3

Elite National

Dominant within their sphere. Enormous audiences and cultural weight in their home regions.

e.g. Super Bowl, NBA Finals, The Ashes

Tier 4

Major

Significant and growing. Strong traditions, dedicated fanbases, and genuine competitive importance.

e.g. Six Nations, Indian Premier League, WTA Finals

Tier 5

Niche

Beloved but limited reach. Passionate communities and rich traditions within their sport.

e.g. World Darts, Snooker World Championship

Did You Know?

Quick Facts

The world of championships is full of staggering numbers and fascinating stories. Here are some of our favourites.

🌐

1.5+ Billion Viewers

The FIFA World Cup final is watched by over 1.5 billion people worldwide — nearly one in five humans on Earth tuning in to the same event at the same time.

🎾

Since 1877

Wimbledon has been played since 1877, making it the oldest tennis tournament in the world. Its grass courts, all-white dress code, and royal patronage are iconic.

🏈

The Halftime Show

The Super Bowl halftime show gets more viewers than most TV series finales. It is a cultural event in its own right, featuring the biggest names in music.

🚴

3,500+ Kilometres

The Tour de France covers 3,500+ km over 23 days, making it one of the most physically demanding championship events in the history of sport.

🏅

10,000+ Athletes

The Olympics feature 10,000+ athletes from 200+ nations competing across more than 30 sports. Nothing else in world sport comes close in scope or scale.

🎶

The Anthem

The Champions League anthem — an adaptation of Handel's "Zadok the Priest" — is one of the most recognizable songs in sport. It sends shivers down spines every matchday.
