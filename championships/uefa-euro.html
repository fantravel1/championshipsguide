What It Is

Continental Football Excellence

UEFA Euro is the European football championship. Held every four years, 16-24 European national teams compete in a knockout tournament. It is the pinnacle of European football.

France, Germany, Italy, Spain, England, Netherlands ‚Äî these nations have defined Euro. The rivalries are intense. The fan bases are passionate. The matches are dramatic.

Why it matters: Winning Euro validates European dominance. Italy's one-time triumph in 2020 (held in 2021). France's consecutive runs. Spain's dominance in the 2010s. These shape continental pride.

Format

Euro Tournament Structure

Group Stage

16-24 teams divided into groups. Each team plays group matches. Top teams advance to knockouts.

Knockout Rounds

Quarterfinals, Semifinals, Final. Single-elimination. Lose and you go home. Extra time and penalty shootouts determine winners if needed.

The Final

Two nations battling for continental supremacy. 90 minutes plus possible extra time. One match determines Euro champion.

Culture

Euro's Greatest Rivalries

Germany vs Netherlands: Intense rivalry defined Euro. Germany vs any team is prestige.

France vs Italy: Continental powerhouses. Matches are always dramatic.

Iconic Moments: Greece's 2004 Euro win (50-1 underdog). Italy's 2020 penalty shootout victory (held in 2021). Zinedine Zidane's headers. Cristiano Ronaldo's performances.

Travel

Attending Euro

Ticket Difficulty: Hard

Euro tickets are precious. Finals can reach $500-$2,000+. Group matches $50-$400. Home nation fans have priority.
