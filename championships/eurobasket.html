What It Is

Europe's Basketball Pride

EuroBasket is Europe's basketball championship. Held every four years, 24 European national teams compete for continental supremacy. France, Spain, Serbia are powerhouses. Germany, Italy, Greece also field strong teams.

European basketball has grown dramatically. Modern EuroBasket features world-class players and competitive depth.

Why it matters: EuroBasket championship titles validate European basketball excellence. These victories mean continental prestige.

Format

Tournament Structure

Group Stage & Knockouts

24 teams divided into groups. Top teams advance to knockout rounds. Quarterfinals, Semifinals, Final.

Best-of-Three Finals

Finals are sometimes extended series, though modern EuroBasket often uses single-game finals.

European Dominance

France, Spain, Serbia have dominated recent EuroBasket tournaments. These nations represent European basketball excellence.
