What It Is

Golf's Most Prestigious Major

The Masters is golf's most prestigious championship. Held every April at Augusta National Golf Club in Georgia, it is one of four major championships (Masters, US Open, Open Championship, PGA Championship).

The Masters is famous for its traditions: the green jacket awarded to the winner, Amen Corner (one of golf's most famous holes), and the sense of exclusivity and elegance.

Why it matters: Winning the Masters defines golf careers. Jack Nicklaus's six Masters titles. Tiger Woods's 15 major championships (5 at the Masters). These victories are treasured.

Format

The Masters Tournament Structure

Invitation Only

Only 96 golfers are invited to play. Top ranked players, past champions, tour winners, and amateurs qualify. The field is elite and limited.

72 Holes (Four Rounds)

Each golfer plays 18 holes per day for four days. Lowest score wins. Ties are resolved in sudden-death playoffs.

The Green Jacket

The winner is presented with a green jacket ‚Äî the most iconic prize in golf. The jacket becomes part of golfing history.

Amen Corner

Holes 11, 12, and 13 are called Amen Corner. One of the most famous sequences in golf. Miracles and disasters happen here.

Culture

Masters Traditions & Iconic Moments

The Green Jacket: Worn only by winners and members of Augusta National. The green jacket is the most prestigious jacket in sports.

Tiger's Comeback (2019): After 11 years without a major, Tiger Woods won the Masters again. The entire golf world erupted. His green jacket redemption was the greatest comeback story in sports.

Jack Nicklaus's Legacy: Six Masters wins, the most in tournament history. Jack is the Masters.

The Par-3 Contest: The day before the tournament, players compete in a fun par-3 contest. Winners get cash, but it's tradition.

Travel

Attending the Masters

Ticket Difficulty: Extreme

Masters tickets are extraordinarily hard to obtain. Most badges go to members and their guests. Resale prices can reach $3,000-$10,000+ per day.

The lottery for general public tickets is competitive. Only the luckiest fans win the chance to attend.

The Solution: Book accommodations in Augusta and enjoy the atmosphere. Watch from practice rounds if possible (slightly easier access).
