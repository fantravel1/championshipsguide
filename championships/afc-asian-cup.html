What It Is

Asia's Football Championship

The AFC Asian Cup is Asia's primary football championship. Held every four years, 24 teams from across Asia compete. Japan, South Korea, Iran, China, Saudi Arabia, and others represent the continent's football excellence.

Asia's football has grown dramatically in quality and investment. The AFC Asian Cup now features world-class teams competing at high levels.

Why it matters: Winning the Asian Cup validates continental dominance. Japan's consistent success. Iran's unexpected runs. Saudi Arabia's traditional strength. South Korea's quality. These shape Asian pride.
