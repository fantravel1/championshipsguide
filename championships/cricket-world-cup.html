What It Is

Cricket's Global Championship

The ICC Cricket World Cup is international cricket's premier championship. Held every four years, it features the world's best cricket nations competing in the One Day International (ODI) format ‚Äî 50 overs per side.

Cricket is the second-most popular sport on Earth, with billions of fans primarily in India, Pakistan, Australia, the Caribbean, South Africa, and Sri Lanka. The World Cup is watched by over 2 billion people globally.

Why it matters: Winning the World Cup validates a cricket nation's dominance. Australia's five titles, India's multiple runs, Pakistan's dramatic victories ‚Äî these shape regional pride and sporting legacy for decades.

Format

The World Cup Structure

Group Stage

10-14 teams divided into groups. Each team plays every other team in their group once. Top teams advance to knockout rounds.

Knockout Rounds

Semifinals and Final. Single-elimination format. One bad match and you're eliminated. Highest-scoring team wins in regulation, or Super Over tiebreakers occur.

The Final

A single match between two champions. 50 overs per side. First to score the most runs wins. Watched by 100+ million people simultaneously in India alone.

Culture

Iconic Cricket Moments

India's 2011 Win: India defeated Sri Lanka in the final at home in front of millions. Sachin Tendulkar's emotional moment. The entire nation celebrated.

Australia's Dominance: Won back-to-back World Cups in 1999 and 2003. Steve Waugh's leadership defined an era.

Pakistan's Drama: Won in 1992 in controversial circumstances. Imran Khan captained the team. Pakistan celebrated their finest sporting moment.

The Super Over: 2019 final between England and New Zealand ended in a tied super over. England won on boundary count. Most dramatic ending in World Cup history.

Travel

Attending the Cricket World Cup

Ticket Difficulty: Very Hard

World Cup cricket tickets are hard to obtain, especially in India. Finals can reach $500-$2,000+. Group matches are more accessible at $50-$300.

In India, matches sell out instantly due to massive demand. In other nations, tickets are slightly more available.
