What It Is

America's Pastime Championship

The World Series is professional baseball's annual championship. The winner of the American League playoffs meets the winner of the National League playoffs in a best-of-seven series every October.

The World Series has been played since 1903 (with exceptions), making it one of the longest-running annual championships in sports. The trophy is small but iconic.

Why it matters: In baseball-obsessed cities (Boston, New York, St. Louis, Chicago), the World Series is the height of regional pride. A championship brings parades and eternal bragging rights. The Yankees' 27 titles (most in sports) define the franchise's dominance.

Format

The World Series Structure

Regular Season (March-October)

All 30 teams play 162 games. Division winners and wild cards are determined. Standings matter year-round.

Playoffs (October)

10 teams qualify (3 division winners + wild cards from each league). Best-of-five divisional series, best-of-seven league championship series, then the World Series.

The World Series (Early-Mid October)

Best-of-seven between league champions. First to 4 wins takes the title. Games alternate between the two stadiums. The intensity builds with each game.

The Parade

The winning team returns home to a city-wide celebration. Fans line the streets. The trophy is displayed. Local pride is maximized.

Culture

Legendary World Series Moments

The Curse of the Bambino: The Boston Red Sox lost 86 years of World Series championships after trading Babe Ruth in 1920. The curse ended in 2004, and Boston went on to win 4 more titles (2004, 2007, 2013, 2018).

The Miracle at Shea Stadium (1969): The New York Mets, an expansion team, won the World Series in their 8th season. Complete underdog story.

Kirk Gibson's Home Run (1988): Dodgers vs Athletics. Gibson, limping on an injured leg, hits a dramatic go-ahead home run in Game 1. Moment defined the series.

The Yankees Dynasty: 27 championships, the most of any team in baseball. From Ruth and Gehrig to Mantle to Jeter, Yankees history is World Series history.

Travel

Attending the World Series

Ticket Difficulty: Very Hard

World Series tickets are expensive and limited. Home fans have priority. Games sell for $200-$2,000+ depending on game number and importance. Game 7 (if played) can reach $5,000+.

Most availability comes through season ticket holders or the secondary market at inflated prices.

The Strategy: If your team makes the Series, apply for lottery tickets through the official team site. Be flexible with game selection. Away Games 3-5 are slightly easier than home Games 1-2, 6-7.
