Tier 4: Prestigious

Tier 4: Prestigious

Well-established championships with strong traditions and smaller but passionate followings. FA Cup, Giro d'Italia, national championships. Significant prestige within devoted fan communities.

Definition

What is Tier 4?

Tier 4 championships are established, traditional competitions with strong legacies and passionate fan bases, but smaller global reach than higher tiers. The FA Cup has been played since 1871, but reaches fewer viewers globally than Champions League. The Giro d'Italia is cycling's second Grand Tour but trails the Tour de France in viewership.

Tier 4 Examples

  • FA Cup (English football)
  • Giro d'Italia (Italian cycling Grand Tour)
  • Vuelta a España (Spanish cycling Grand Tour)
  • Snooker World Championship
  • Wimbledon Doubles (Secondary to Singles)
  • National Football/Soccer Cups worldwide
