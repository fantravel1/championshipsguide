Tier 3: Elite National

Tier 3: Elite National

Dominant championships within single nations or regions. Super Bowl, World Series, Copa America, UEFA Euro. Massive viewership and impact within their geographic scope, but primarily national or continental rather than truly global.

Definition

Tier 3 Characteristics

Tier 3 championships are the most significant within their nation or region. The Super Bowl is North America's single biggest cultural event. Copa America is South America's football championship. These championships have massive viewership and cultural impact, but are primarily national or continental rather than truly global.
