Definition

Tier 5: Completing the Picture

Tier 5 championships include niche sports, emerging competitions, and regional championships with deep historical roots or growing significance. These complete the comprehensive landscape of competitive sport worldwide. From badminton world championships to table tennis nationals, curling to weightlifting, these championships matter deeply to their communities.

Tier 5 is the largest category because sport encompasses hundreds of disciplines. These championships may have millions of passionate followers within their sport, but smaller global reach than Tier 1-4.

Tier 5 Characteristics

  • Niche sports (badminton, table tennis, weightlifting, gymnastics)
  • Emerging esports championships (growing rapidly)
  • Regional championships (state/provincial competitions)
  • Specialized sport divisions (women's-only, youth championships)
  • Historic but smaller-audience competitions
