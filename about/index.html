About ChampionshipsGuide

The definitive reference for world championships across every sport.

Our Story

Why We Built This

Championships are the moments that define sport. They're the reason we set alarms for 3am kick-offs, plan entire vacations around fixture calendars, and remember exactly where we were when that goal/point/try/ace happened.

ChampionshipsGuide was born from a simple frustration: there was no single place that catalogued, compared, and celebrated every major championship in sport. Wikipedia has the facts. ESPN has the highlights. But nobody was asking the bigger questions: Why does this championship matter more than that one? What makes a Tier 1 event different from a Tier 3 event? How do you actually get tickets?

We built ChampionshipsGuide to be that resource — a comprehensive, lovingly crafted reference for fans who care about the biggest moments in sport.

Our Mission

Three Pillars

Everything we do at ChampionshipsGuide comes back to three core goals.

📚

Catalogue

Document every significant championship across every major sport, from the FIFA World Cup to the World Darts Championship. We believe every championship has a story worth telling — and fans deserve a single place to find them all.

⚖

Compare

Our proprietary 5-tier rating system helps fans understand relative prestige and significance. Is the Champions League bigger than the Super Bowl? How does the Cricket World Cup compare to the Rugby World Cup? Our tiers answer those questions.

🌏

Connect

Help fans attend these events through travel guides, ticket advice, and city profiles. Knowing about a championship is one thing — being there when the champion is crowned is something else entirely.

Our Methodology

The Tier System

How we rate and compare championships across every sport.

At the heart of ChampionshipsGuide is our proprietary 5-tier rating system. We rate every championship based on four criteria:

🌎 Global Audience

How many people watch this championship worldwide? Does it transcend borders and languages?

🏆 Historical Prestige

How long has this championship existed? What is its legacy? How deep is its history?

⚽ Competitive Depth

How strong is the field? Are the best athletes and teams in the world competing?

🌈 Cultural Significance

How deeply does this championship resonate beyond sport? Does it shape culture and identity?

Based on these four criteria, every championship is assigned one of five tiers:

Tier Label Description
Tier 1 Pinnacle The absolute summit of sport. Global events that transcend competition and become cultural phenomena.
Tier 2 Premier Elite championships with massive global audiences, deep history, and world-class competition.
Tier 3 Elite Highly prestigious championships that dominate their sport or region.
Tier 4 Established Respected championships with strong followings and competitive fields.
Tier 5 Niche Smaller or emerging championships that matter deeply to dedicated fan communities.

What We Believe

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we publish.

🛡

Independence

No governing body tells us how to rate championships. Our opinions are our own. We are not funded by, affiliated with, or beholden to any sports organization, broadcaster, or league. This independence is non-negotiable — it is the foundation of our credibility.

🌎

Global Perspective

We cover championships worldwide, not just from a US or European lens. Cricket matters as much as baseball. The Rugby World Cup matters as much as the Super Bowl. We assess championships on their global significance, not through the bias of any single continent or culture.

❤

Fan-First

Everything is written for fans, by fans. No corporate jargon. No press-release language. We write the way fans talk about sport — with passion, opinion, and a deep love for the drama of championships. If it reads like it was written by a committee, we rewrite it.

🔍

Accuracy

We fact-check dates, venues, formats, and history obsessively. Every championship profile is researched, verified, and updated regularly. Sport deserves the same editorial rigor as any other subject — and our readers deserve to trust every detail they find here.
